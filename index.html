FAQ

Common questions

What does "placement guarantee" mean?
If you complete the Placement Bundle — all modules, projects, and mock interviews — and don't get placed within 6 months, we refund 100% of your fee. No questions asked.
Do I need prior knowledge to enroll?
No. Every course starts from scratch. You need a laptop, an internet connection, and the commitment to show up consistently.
How long do I have access to the course?
Lifetime access. Once you purchase, the content — including any future updates — is yours forever.
Is the certificate recognised by companies?
Yes. Our certificates are recognised by 4,000+ hiring partners across India including Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Amazon, and many mid-size product companies.
Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund on all individual courses. For the Placement Bundle, see the placement guarantee policy above.
